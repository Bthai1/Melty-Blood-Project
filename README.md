## Controls

### Player 1:

Jump: W 

Crouch: S

Move Left: A

Move Right: D

Attack A: U

Attack B: I

Attack C: O

### Player 2:

Jump: ArrowUp

Crouch: ArrowDown

Move Left: ArrowLeft

Move Right: ArrowRight

Attack A: Numpad 1

Attack B: Numpad 2

Attack C: Numpad 3
